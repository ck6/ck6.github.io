FAQ

1. Are these rewards real? Can I withdraw them?
Yes! The tokens you collect (BTC, USDT, TON) are genuine and accumulate in your account. You can see your balances in the Wallet tab. Withdrawals happen weekly, provided you meet the minimum balance of $20.
2. How do I reveal tiles?
You need cookies (or other sweets) to reveal each tile. Tap a hex tile, and if you have at least one cookie in your inventory, that tile will flip to show any reward hidden beneath.
3. I ran out of cookies. What should I do?
If you don’t have any cookies, a popup will appear directing you to the Store tab. Head there to buy more cookies (or candy, or bombs) so you can keep unveiling tiles.
4. How do I earn more cookies or unlock bigger items?
Go to the Store tab to purchase various items using Telegram’s built-in payment system. Some items require you to invite friends first—check your Bonuses tab to see how many invites you need to unlock them.
5. How do I invite friends?
In the Bonuses tab, there’s an Invite button and a Copy Link button. Share your personal link with friends on Telegram or anywhere else. Each new friend you invite helps you unlock additional items (and sometimes free cookies!).
6. Why connect a TON wallet?
Connecting your TON wallet allows you to withdraw TON directly to your personal address. If you’d like to receive TON rewards off-platform, simply press the Connect button under the Wallet tab and follow the prompts.
7. What is the “Chonk” and how does feeding work?
The Chonk is our friendly mascot. Each time you uncover rewards, you “feed” the Chonk. As you feed it more, you level up and increase your overall bonus on winnings. Check the Bonuses tab or the feeding progress bar to see your current level and bonus.
8. Can I select a specific cluster?
Yes! In the Treasure tab (the main field), tap Select Cluster at the bottom and enter a cluster number. Clusters range from 1 to 3,333,334. You can also load a random one anytime by tapping Random Cluster.
9. Where can I get more help or support?
If you have any questions or run into any issues, tap the Support link in the Wallet tab or message @luckychonksupport on Telegram.
